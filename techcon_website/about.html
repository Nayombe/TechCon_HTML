Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for software developers and has grown into one of the world's most influential technology conferences. Each year, it gathers thousands of tech enthusiasts to explore emerging technologies, industry trends, and collaborative innovations.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the global tech community through education, connection, and innovation. We strive to build a platform where ideas flourish, networks expand, and careers ignite.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted a lineup of remarkable speakers who've shaped the future of technology:

Dr. Ken Mande

AI pioneer and author of “Ethics in the Machine Age.” She spoke at TechCon 2021 about the future of responsible AI.

Nayombe Syntax

Founder of NeuralWeb and advocate for open-source brain-computer interfaces. Presented at TechCon 2022 on deep learning trends.

George Stack

CTO of KernelWare and key contributor to decentralized internet standards. His 2023 talk on Web3 inspired thousands.
